Hi, I'm Ruturaj Pradhan

I'm a one-person creative studio, passionate about telling powerful stories through both visuals and words. With a blend of technical skill and artistic vision, I offer a full spectrum of services—from photography and video production to cinematic film direction and script development. Every project I take on is more than just a job—it's a collaboration. Whether I’m capturing the fleeting beauty of a candid moment, filming a branded short, or writing a script from scratch, I pour my creativity and attention into every detail. I believe in storytelling that resonates—stories that not only look beautiful but leave a lasting emotional impact. Photography lets me freeze time and capture the essence of a moment. Videography allows me to bring movement, mood, and rhythm into visual storytelling. Filmography gives me the space to think bigger—conceptualizing, directing, and editing cinematic experiences. And scriptwriting is where it all begins: crafting the narrative foundation that drives every great story. Whether you're a brand looking for authentic content, an artist with a vision to share, a couple seeking to preserve a once-in-a-lifetime memory, or a fellow creative in need of a trusted collaborator—I’m here to bring your ideas to life with care, clarity, and creativity.
